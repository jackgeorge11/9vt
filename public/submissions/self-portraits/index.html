call to submissions: self-portraits

our Moods series began when our dear friend Juju discovered ten self-portraits she'd made during 2020; to us they felt moody, like each portrayed a different a part of her over the course of her time making them.

we liked the collection and the idea behind it, so we decided to sell it.

using the term 'self-portrait' loosely, we urge other artists to submit work (preferably a series) that represents themselves.

we leave any further interpretation of the prompt up to you.

submit your work or any questions you might have to submissions@9vtbackslash5.com.
